PROFILE

I am an architecture graduate with a strong interest in urban design and 3 years of exciting work experience in urban praxis. I’ve also travelled to several cities in Europe and Asia which enriched my perspective of good urban settings. I’m currently pursuing graduate study in City Planning to achieve comprehensive and advance understanding in urbanism. I am excited to learn about big data and visualization to complete my journey at MIT.

Academic/Research Interest: Urban Design, Walkability, Transit Oriented Development,Public space, Placemaking, Landscape Design

Inspiring Visualization Project

THE CARBON MAP

The Carbon Map is an interactive map that help to convey how different countries fit into the climate change picture. I find it interesting because it narrates an important issue, such as climate change, in a unique or playful display. Without exhibiting overwhelming stats, the Carbon Map is able to spatially communicate the significant impact of climate change. It is created by Duncan Clark and Robin Houston. Check it out here
